6. Children's privacy

The App does not use cookies. We do not use cross-site tracking technologies. Any analytics we collect are aggregated and do not personally identify you.

8. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, Row-Level Security (RLS) policies in our database, and restricted access controls. However, no transmission method or electronic storage system is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Push notifications

Push notifications (e.g., daily fortune alerts, lucky time reminders) are sent only after you grant explicit permission. You can withdraw this permission at any time through your device's notification settings.

10. International data transfers
