Account Management

Account Deletion Guide

Learn how to delete your K-Saju account and personal data.

📱
Delete in the App (Recommended)
  1. 1
    Open the K-Saju app and go to Settings from the bottom tab bar.
  2. 2
    Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap the Delete Account button.
  3. 3
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Tap Delete Account to confirm.
✉️
Request via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer a manual request, contact us by email.

Email address

support@k-saju.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request
🗑️
Data That Will Be Deleted
  • Profile and account information
  • Saju readings and fortune history
  • Wellness diary entries
  • Relationship and compatibility data
  • App settings and preferences
📋
Data That Will Be Retained
  • Transaction records — retained for 5 years as required by Korean E-Commerce Act (Article 6)
⏱️
Timeline
In-app deletion: Processed immediately.
Email request: Processed within 30 days.
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
