What is 사주 (Saju)?

Saju (사주, 四柱) means "Four Pillars." At the exact moment you were born, the cosmos aligned — Year, Month, Day, and Hour each carry a Heavenly Stem and Earthly Branch, forming eight characters (팔자, Paljja) that map your entire life's journey.

Unlike Western astrology focused on a single rising sun sign, Saju weaves five elemental forces — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water — through 60-year cycles, revealing not just personality but timing: when to act, when to wait, when fortune favors.

Practiced across Korea for millennia, Saju was the language of kings, generals, and matchmakers. Today, it's the daily guide of millions — and with K-Saju, it belongs to the world.

年 1st Pillar
Year Pillar — Origin

Shaped by the year of birth. Reveals ancestral karma, social reputation, and early life destiny. Governed by the 12 zodiac animals.

月 2nd Pillar
Month Pillar — Growth

Born of the birth month. Governs parents, siblings, and career foundation — the pillars of mid-life ambition.

日 3rd Pillar
Day Pillar — Self

The most personal pillar. The Day Stem is your true self — your core nature, marriage energy, and inner world. This is who you truly are.

時 4th Pillar
Hour Pillar — Legacy

Cast by the birth hour. Illuminates children, later life, and the legacy you leave behind. The final chapter of your story.

The Five Elements · 오행
木 火 土 金 水

Wood · Fire · Earth · Metal · Water. These five forces flow through every being and every moment. Balance them, and life flows. Imbalance invites friction — and opportunity.
